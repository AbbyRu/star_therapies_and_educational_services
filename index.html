Who We Are

A Specialist Team Dedicated to Children

We are a team of specialists providing Educational and Occupational Therapy Assessments and Interventions, and Special Educational Needs (SEN) Consultations. We work alongside families and educational settings to enable children and young people to reach their full potential.

We believe in a holistic, child-centred approach. Our work focuses not just on addressing challenges but on building confidence and self-reliance ‚Äî because every child brings their own unique strengths.
